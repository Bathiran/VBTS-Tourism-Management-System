Our Story

Before we formed VBTS Tourism Center, our "center" worked for an interactive agency, which is where we first met. Like others with an entrepreneurial drive, we evaluated ourselves and decided to go it alone as a design and development team. Tourer is the first WordPress theme we've created. Despite the refusal, we persisted, and eventually it was accepted. After the success of that venture, we decided to quit our full-time jobs and start our own business. As a result, VBTSGroup was born. It was created in 2020. Now it is becoming a growing entity.
